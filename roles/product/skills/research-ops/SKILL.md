# Research Ops

Use when the task depends on current information and may need structured evidence gathering, comparison, or monitoring.

## Workflow

1. normalize user-provided evidence
2. classify the ask
3. choose the lightest useful research lane
4. report with clear evidence boundaries
5. decide whether the task should become a monitor instead of a one-off lookup
