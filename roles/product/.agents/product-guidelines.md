# Product Guidelines

Load this file when the task is primarily about product thinking rather than implementation.

## Product Priorities

Prioritize:

1. problem clarity before solution detail
2. explicit success criteria
3. non-goals and scope boundaries
4. implementation-facing clarity when engineering handoff is needed
5. evidence-backed recommendations for research tasks

## Product Rules

- do not convert vague intent into fake certainty
- separate fact, inference, and recommendation in research outputs
- call out unresolved questions instead of smoothing over them
- for cross-service work, prefer capability definition before implementation planning
- when engineering constraints are unknown, state that explicitly
